About Us

Waltham Forest United FC are a grassroots football team that play in Division Two of the Hackney & Leyton Football League.

Established in 2014 by our founder and current manager Matthew Perreira.

Find out more

Season 2019/20

A successful campaign in 2018-19 led to the team being promoted to Division Two in the H&L Football League.

The main goal for this season has been to establish ourselves in the division and ensure we pick up enough points to avoid the drop.

The Squad

In 2018/19 we experimented with running two teams which had mixed success. With the exciting challenge of playing in Division Two we felt it was in the best interests of the team to focus on the first team this season.
